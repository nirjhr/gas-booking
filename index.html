About Us

Anila Gas Company is an Indian company supplying LPG cooking gas for consumers all over India. It supplies the gas through distributors. The company has offices in each state which manage all the area offices under it. The area offices manage all distributors in that district. The company has various types of gas cylinders like 5kg, 14.5kg, etc.

Our Operations

Each state office maintains the details about area offices such as unique area number, name, address, manager. State offices have unique names, addresses, and store the total number of area offices, total distributors under each area office, and total sales in each area. The area offices have unique area numbers, names, and total sales, and also maintain distributor details such as unique distributor number, name, address, and proprietor. They also store total sales in that area and sales by each distributor.

Consumer Services

The distributor maintains the details about all the consumers such as unique consumer number, name, address, pin code, contact number, email ID, distributor, number of cylinders, and date of connection. To get a new connection, the consumer should provide their details and submit documents for address proof. On payment of the requisite deposit, the consumer will be issued a Subscription Voucher (SV). The SV contains consumer number, consumer name, address, date of connection, distributor name, deposit amount, type of cylinder, number of cylinders, and distributor name and address.

Booking and Delivery

The consumer can book gas by calling or personally visiting by providing their consumer number. They will be informed about the booking number and the date they will receive the gas. The receiving date of gas will be 5 days after the booking date. The distributor office maintains the stock registry of gas which includes details of cylinder type, price, and quantity. It is updated after supplying gas to the consumer. The area officer transfers cylinders from one distributor to another based on demand.

Connection Transfer

The consumer can transfer their connection to another place. On the consumerâ€™s request, the distributor at the current location will issue a Termination Voucher (TV) and will refund the deposit amount mentioned in the SV, on surrender of existing equipment (cylinders and regulator). The TV is valid for one year from the date of issue. It contains TV number, TV date, consumer number, SV number, SV date, cylinder type, consumer name, address, distributor, transferee distributor, amount paid, and remarks. Reconnection can be availed at the new place by payment of the same deposit amount mentioned in the TV. On receipt of the TV and deposit amount, the distributor sends a request to the area officer for transfer of consumer details. Then a new SV is issued to the consumer.
